# User Research Synthesis

## What
Distilling raw user research (interviews, surveys, usability sessions) into structured insight that drives design decisions.

## How
1. Cluster raw observations into themes (affinity mapping)
2. For each theme: state the finding, supporting evidence, and frequency
3. Extract jobs-to-be-done from behavioral patterns
4. Identify pain points ranked by frequency and severity
5. Derive opportunity areas — gaps between current experience and user goals
6. Document assumptions that need further validation
