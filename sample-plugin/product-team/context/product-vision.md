# Product Vision

Our product helps growth-stage SaaS teams ship better product decisions faster without drowning in process overhead or losing institutional knowledge between cycles.

## North Star
Every product decision is grounded in evidence, written down, and traceable to outcomes.

## Current Focus (Q2 2026)
- Launch the core flow execution engine with support for explore and refine step types
- Deliver the sample plugin as an onboarding accelerator for new teams
- Establish baseline quality metrics: time-to-first-artifact, reviewer convergence rate, and artifact reuse rate
