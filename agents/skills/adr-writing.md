# Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

## What
A short document capturing an architectural decision, its context, the options considered, and the rationale for the chosen approach. ADRs are immutable once accepted — they document history, not intent.

## How
1. State the decision in one sentence (active voice, past tense)
2. Describe the context that forced the decision
3. List options considered with pros/cons — minimum 2
4. State the chosen option and why
5. List consequences — positive and negative
6. Status: proposed / accepted / deprecated / superseded
